Join the Band

The GCB accepts adult musicians as playing members. Anyone interested in joining is encouraged to contact the Manager or the Director.

We currently have openings in some sections of the band, particularly:

  • Woodwinds (Flutes, Clarinets, etc.)
  • Trombones
  • Tubas

The GCB rehearses each Monday evening from 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm in the Multi-Purpose Room of The Greenbelt Community Center
(15 Crescent Rd # 200, Greenbelt, MD 20770).
