The Wind Ensemble of the Greenbelt Concert Band

The Wind Ensemble

The Greenbelt Wind Ensemble is an off-shoot of the Greenbelt Concert Band. The ensemble performs concerts in the City of Greenbelt and surrounding areas on special occasions.

The Wind Ensemble rehearses every Tuesday afternoon in the "Multi-Purpose Room" in the Greenbelt Community Center in Greenbelt, Maryland. The ensemble is open to all those with playing experience.
